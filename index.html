The Relationship Reflection

This short reflection helps us understand what you’re hoping for during your time at Wild Forest Estate. Whether you’re here to reconnect, celebrate, or simply pause together, your answers help us hold space well. You can complete it together or separately. There are no right answers.

1. How would you describe your relationship right now?

Why this matters: It helps us meet you where you are, not where you think you should be.

Examples
  • We’re feeling close, but a little tired from life lately.
  • We’re in a good place, but want to deepen our connection.

2. What do you most appreciate about your partner or your relationship?

Why this matters: Appreciation helps us remember what’s working and what’s worth celebrating.

Examples
  • We laugh together, even when things are hard.
  • We support each other’s dreams.

3. Is there a quality, feeling, or experience you’d love to have more of during your stay?

Why this matters: Naming your hopes helps us create space for them.

Examples
  • More playfulness and lightness.
  • Time to talk without distractions.

4. Is there anything you’d like to leave behind or have less of during your retreat?

Why this matters: Letting go makes room for what matters most.

Examples
  • Stress from work and daily routines.
  • Our tendency to check our phones too much.

5. What would make this retreat feel truly meaningful for you?

Why this matters: Your definition of meaning helps us shape your experience.

Examples
  • Feeling reconnected and understood.
  • Creating a new memory together.


Only fill in your name and email if you'd like us to follow up (gently of course). You are welcome to leave it blank.
